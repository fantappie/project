Project Proposal

By Alex Fantappie

What is the website about?

This will be a personal professional website about me. I will outline my passions, my background, and the projects I am currently working on, specifically my startup, Avanti.

What is the objective?

the objective of the site will be to share information about myself on the web for networking and career purposes.

Who is target audience?

The target audience for the website is 20-30 year old professionals in the United States who could be intereted in working with me or getting in touch with me. Other possible users could be future colleagues and investors in my startup.

What content do you plan to include in each page of your site?

Page One: (about)

A page that includes a brief overview about me, some images of myself and the images I care about, a short bio, and a resume-type section that outlines my strengths and accomplishments. Will also have links to my Twitter, LinkedIn, Facebook

Page Two (fun stuff):

A page that provides background information about me, including where I grew up and where I'm currently located, what my hobbies and interests are, and links to essays and videos that I have learned from and want to share with others.

Page Three (projects):

A page that outlines my current and past professional endeavors. It will outline info about my startup, avanti, as well as past startups I've created, my current investments, etc.

Page Four (places):

A page that shows the places I have traveled in my life and the images I have taken from those trips. Will include images and descriptions from photos I have taken in Italy, Spain, France, Japan, Hong Kong, and more.
